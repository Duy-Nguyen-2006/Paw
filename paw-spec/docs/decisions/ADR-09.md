# ADR-09: Design reference catalog

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-06-16
- **Deciders:** Tech Lead, Design Eng, Legal

## Context
Design clarification benefits from reference links, but scraping or cloning third-party designs
creates legal and quality risk.

## Decision
Ship a **small, static, curated** catalog of direction-setting references. **No scraping, no
cloning, no copying** of branding/assets/copy.

## Consequences
- (+) Bounded, legally safe, predictable.
- (-) Catalog must be maintained manually.

## Revisit trigger
The catalog grows enough to warrant its own service.
